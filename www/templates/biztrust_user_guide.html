USER GUIDE
1. Target Users

Any individual or company who wishes to verify the validity of the copy BizTrust info from Suruhanjaya Syarikat Malaysia (SSM).

2. How the apps work

I.
The SSM BizTrust QR Code screen will display a "Tap screen to scan" message to enable scanning of the QR Code.
II.
After running the QR Code scanner user must point camera towards the QR code given. Tap 'x' at a convenient position to redirect to the front page.

3. Output

I.
Corporate information will be displayed if the QR code successfully scanning upon the QR code. Tap the QR icon located at a convenient position to redirect to SSM BizTrust QR code page.
II.
An "Invalid SSM BizTrust QR Code" will prompt if the QR Code is not valid. Tap the QR icon located at convenient position to redirect to SSM BizTrust QR code page.
III.
The app will display "Information Not Found" if the entity has not registered with SSM. Tap the QR icon located at a convenient position to redirect to SSM BizTrust QR code page.
IV.
The app will display "Connection Lost" if there is no internet connection. Tap the QR icon located at a convenient position to redirect to SSM BizTrust QR code page.

4. Verify

Users have to use MySSM app to verify the BizTrust QR code.
